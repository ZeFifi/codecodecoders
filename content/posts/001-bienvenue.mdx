## What is a Digital Garden?

A digital garden is a different approach to content creation and knowledge sharing. Unlike traditional blogs with their chronological posts, a digital garden is more like a wiki - interconnected, ever-evolving, and growing organically.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Notes and Essays**: Thoughts on technology, programming, and personal development
- **Learning in Public**: Documentation of my learning journey
- **Work in Progress**: Unpolished ideas and ongoing projects

Feel free to explore, and watch this space grow over time. The content here will be regularly updated and refined as my understanding deepens and evolves.
